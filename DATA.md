# Data

## Source

HuggingFace dataset: ashraq/fashion-product-images-small
- 44,000 fashion product images with metadata
- Streamed (not fully downloaded) to save disk space

## Filtering

- Kept only subCategory == "Tshirts" -> 700 images
- Further filtered to 3 pattern classes (Printed, Striped, Solid)
- Melange (3) and Abstract (1) dropped due to insufficient samples

## Weak Label Parsing

Labels extracted from productDisplayName field using keyword matching:

- color: matched against 21 known color keywords
- pattern: matched against Printed/Striped/Solid/Melange/Abstract keywords
- sleeve: not extractable from titles (0% coverage) — dropped
- neck: 30.8% coverage, mostly Unknown — dropped

## Label Quality

| Attribute | Coverage | Classes |
|-----------|----------|---------|
| color     | 100%     | 21      |
| pattern   | 100%     | 3       |

## Splits

| Split          | Count | Notes                           |
|----------------|-------|---------------------------------|
| labeled_train  | 180   | used for LoRA training          |
| labeled_test   | 47    | held-out, never used in training|
| unlabeled_pool | 469   | used for pseudo-labeling        |

## Pseudo-Labels

- Generated by LoRA-180 model on unlabeled_pool
- Confidence threshold: 0.95
- Accepted: 255 (54.4%) | Rejected: 214 (45.6%)
- Pseudo-labels not verified manually — treated as noisy silver labels
